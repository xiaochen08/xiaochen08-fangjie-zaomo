## Opening menu

Translate this compact menu into the user's language and ask them to choose one number/name or describe a combination:

1. 生物与角色
2. 武器与战斗装置
3. 穿戴与角色装备
4. 物品、资源与消耗品
5. 方块与世界组件
6. 机器、红石与自动化
7. 建筑与大型结构
8. 家具与室内陈设
9. 载具与坐骑
10. 植物、地形与环境物件
11. 魔法、投射物与特效载体
12. 技术、展示与特殊模型
13. 不确定／组合类型：直接描述用途，由 Codex 帮助归类

If the user already names a specific subject, route it automatically and confirm the inferred family instead of repeating the whole menu. After selection, show only the relevant subcategories below. Accept multi-family projects such as a mounted boss, weaponized vehicle, animated furniture, or magical machine.

A category choice is not design approval. Continue the full brief, texture recommendation, three concept previews, and explicit approval gate.

## Entities and characters

- Players, custom skins as geometry, humanoid NPCs, villagers, merchants, guards, soldiers, bandits, companions, quest givers, class characters, and mannequins.
- Passive creatures: farm animals, wildlife, birds, fish, insects, pets, babies, ambient creatures, and biome variants.
- Neutral or tameable creatures: wolves, cats, mounts, guardians, territorial beasts, familiars, and utility companions.
- Hostile mobs: zombies, skeletons, undead, mutants, arthropods, slimes, aquatic threats, flying enemies, Nether/End creatures, aliens, demons, and corrupted variants.
- Bosses and minibosses: single- or multi-headed beasts, giants, dragons, raid bosses, phase-changing enemies, dungeon guardians, and multipart targets.
- Fantasy and magical life: elementals, spirits, angels, demons, mimics, shapeshifters, summoned beings, and living plants.
- Constructs: golems, statues, robots, drones, mechanical animals, clockwork creatures, turrets with character rigs, and animated armor.

## Gameplay and genre routes

- **Survival and vanilla+**: tools, food, storage, farms, utility blocks, shelters, animals, and restrained Minecraft styling.
- **RPG and adventure**: classes, NPCs, quests, weapons, armor, loot, dungeons, bosses, factions, mounts, and skill projectiles.
- **Redstone and technical Minecraft**: automation, farm shells, storage, logic interfaces, sensors, transport, and readable machine states.
- **Technology and industry**: energy, processing, logistics, factories, robots, vehicles, science fiction, steampunk, dieselpunk, or cyberpunk.
- **Magic and fantasy**: spellcasting, mana systems, artifacts, mythical creatures, temples, portals, elemental themes, and summoning.
- **Horror and dark fantasy**: corrupted mobs, parasites, undead, occult props, haunted furniture, ruins, and transformation rigs.
- **Military and warfare**: soldiers, armor, firearms, vehicles, fortifications, turrets, artillery, drones, and tactical props.
- **Decoration and role-play**: furniture, shops, costumes, food, city props, professions, minigames, social spaces, and themed interiors.
- Farming/ecology, exploration/dimensions, PvP/minigames, education/science, post-apocalypse, and mixed-genre routes are also valid.

## Scope boundaries

- A large house or city may be better delivered as a Minecraft build/schematic or modular kit than one `.bbmodel`; clarify the intended runtime and placement method.
- Real redstone logic, AI, damage, inventory, crafting, particles, shaders, sound, and projectile behavior are runtime systems. Model their visible carriers and document integration events separately.
- Pure 2D GUI art, skins without added geometry, textures without a 3D asset, world generation, and code-only mechanics are adjacent tasks, not Blockbench model production.
- This taxonomy is extensible, not a restriction. Route any free-text description to the nearest family or a cross-family brief.
